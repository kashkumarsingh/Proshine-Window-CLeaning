About ProShine Window Cleaning

ProShine Window Cleaning is a local, family-owned business run by three brothers who,after graduating from Northwest Nazarene University, decided to stay in the Boise area to enjoy all four of Southwest Idaho's beautiful seasons and abundance of outdoor activities.

Together, we created a professional, ethical company providing excellent service to our customers. We thrive on creating personal relationships with our customers. We offer residential and commercial window cleaning and pressure washing services, gutter cleaning, and Christmas light installation, including holiday displays, in Meridian, Kuna, Eagle, and the surrounding areas.
